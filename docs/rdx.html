FAQ

What is rdx?

rdx for Reddit is a lightweight, read-only app for Reddit, initially inspired by the Apollo app. It provides a clean and minimalistic interface designed for fast browsing without ads.

How do I access rdx for Reddit?

You can access rdx for Reddit web app by visiting rdx.overdevs.com or downloading the rdx for Reddit app for Android and iOS. It is optimized for both mobile and desktop use.

What features does rdx for Reddit app offer?

  • Ad/Pop-up Free Browsing: Enjoy a clutter-free experience.
  • Mobile Optimization: Works smoothly on lower-end smartphones and desktops.
  • Import Subscriptions: Easily import your Reddit subscriptions for a personalized feed.
  • Save Posts: Bookmark your favorite posts for later access.
  • Enhanced Video/GIF Playback: Supports various video formats from multiple providers like Reddit, Giphy, Imgur, Gfycat for smooth playback.
  • Improved Search: Quickly find content with an enhanced search feature.
  • Compact Mode: A compact mode for viewing more content in a small space. You can easily click the thumbnails to open the media without waiting for loading.
  • Infinite Scrolling: Scroll through your feed without interruptions.
  • Custom Themes and Fonts: Personalize your viewing experience with multiple dark and light themes and 20+ fonts.

Are there mobile apps available for rdx?

Yes, rdx for Reddit has mobile applications available for both Android and iOS:

  • Android App: rdx for Reddit is available on the Google Play Store. Users can download it for a seamless browsing experience on Android devices. The app supports features like subreddit switching and native video playback from platforms like Imgur and Giphy.
  • iOS App: The rdx app is available on the iOS App Store. It offers features such as compact and full post preview modes, native support for videos, GIFs, and galleries, all while maintaining a user-friendly interface.

Can I log in to my Reddit account using rdx for Reddit?

No, rdx app is a read-only viewer and does not support logging in or posting content.

Is rdx free to use?

Yes,rdx is completely free to use and the web-app is open-source.

What should I do if I encounter issues while using the app?

If you experience problems, such as a blank page, it may be due to VPN usage. You can try disabling CORS with a browser add-on or adjusting your browser's privacy settings. For further assistance, consider checking the browser's console for errors.
Please use the help page in case your issue is already solved or use the contact page to submit your issue.
